Castrol Chicken Run

The wildest car race on the radio, ever. In honour of JJ McClure (Cannonball Run 1981), they put up a bounty and invited like-minded free spirits to break from the monotony of their lives, take the car and hit the open road. The 2008 website is presented here. netbek also produced the 2007 version.
