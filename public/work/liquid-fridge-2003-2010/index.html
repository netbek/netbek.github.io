Liquid Fridge 2003-2010

Liquid Fridge has been through many incarnations from its beginnings as a segment of a radio programme. Throughout, Liquid Fridge has sought to develop South African creative arts in all forms through fostering interaction and mutual support within the creative community. A selection of graphics is presented here. netbek also produced the website and the forum.
